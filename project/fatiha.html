Surah Fatiha is the first Surah in terms of ascending order in Holy Quran. Another name of Surah is Umm ul Quran. The Surah comes with immense benefits and blessings for true believers. Surah is also known as, Umm Al-Kitab ("the Mother of the Book"). The name Fatiha refers to “the opening of the Book”.
